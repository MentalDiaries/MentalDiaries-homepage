Facts
  • Depression, anxiety and behavioural disorders are among the leading causes of illness and disability among children.
  • According to the Indian Journal of Psychiatry in 2019, even before the pandemic, at least 50 million children in India were affected with mental health issues.
  • 80-90% of children experiencing mental health problems fail to address it, thus are unable to receive adequate treatment.
  • According to WHO in 2020, the economic loss due to mental health conditions between 2012-2030 is estimated to be USD 1.03 trillion in India.
